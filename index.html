How to use MASSING

Navigating

Drag to rotate. Right-click drag (or two-finger drag) to pan. Scroll or pinch to zoom.

The compass shows which way you're facing — click it to snap back to north-up. Recenter returns to the default view. Plan View switches to a straight-down, north-up reading of the massing.

Reading a building

Click any building for its real PLUTO record — address, BBL, land use, year built, recorded height and floor count, lot area, and how much of its allowed zoning capacity is actually built.

Color modes

Land Use — the lot's recorded use category. FAR Use — how built-out a lot is relative to what zoning allows there. Year Built — construction era.

Click any item in the legend to hide that category from the view — click again to bring it back. "Show all" / "Hide all" toggles everything at once.

Zoning envelope

The translucent volume above a building is its unbuilt allowed capacity — real lot area × the greatest of its residential, commercial, or community-facility FAR, compared against what's actually built there.

About the data

Real NYC PLUTO tax lot records and Building Footprints, from NYC Open Data — one volume per real lot, not a model. Boroughs load one at a time, capped at 15,000 lots each for performance.
